Detailed Description

An Entity in our game is a 'device' or 'object' (entity, actually) which is made up of various components, which can be thought of as attributes or behaviors. For example, a player, boss, and alien all move. They all have life. Aliens 'spawn' missiles and a player decides to shoot them. A missile moves, but does not move side to side, etc. A static entity, such as a stone or a building, has no life. All entities have a position in the game. As such, they have a center, a size. All entities have a type.
